Our Story

Built for people who
respect their time

PrecisionMeet was born from frustration — the endless back-and-forth of scheduling. We built the tool we always wished existed.

Our Mission

Scheduling should take seconds, not days

Every professional knows the pain: a simple 30-minute meeting turns into a 3-day email chain just to find a time that works. Meanwhile, you're spending mental energy that should be going toward real work.

PrecisionMeet was built to solve exactly this. By combining live calendar integrations with AI-powered recommendations, we take the friction out of scheduling entirely.

Whether you're a VP coordinating cross-functional reviews or a student leader running weekly chapter meetings, PrecisionMeet adapts to how you work.

What We Stand For

Our values

These principles shape every feature we ship and every decision we make.

⚡

Speed Without Sacrifice

We believe fast and thoughtful aren't opposites. PrecisionMeet is designed to get you to a booked meeting in under 60 seconds, without cutting corners on accuracy.

🔒

Privacy First

Your calendar data is yours. We read availability to power scheduling — we never store, share, or analyze your event details. Zero retention, full transparency.

🎯

Intentional Design

Every button, every AI prompt, every notification has a reason. We remove features that don't serve the core goal: getting meaningful meetings booked, fast.

🤝

Built for Real Users

We built PrecisionMeet by talking to actual professionals and student leaders — not hypothetical users. Their workflows drive our roadmap, not the other way around.
