Step by Step

Four steps to a perfectly scheduled meeting

No setup headaches. No manual entry. Just smart scheduling from start to finish.

1

Connect your calendar

Sign in with Google or Microsoft and authorize PrecisionMeet to read your calendar availability. We support Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook — and we only read free/busy data, never the details of your events. Setup takes under 60 seconds.

2

Describe your meeting to the AI

Type a sentence or two about what the meeting is for — a quick project sync, a strategy session, a client onboarding call. The AI reads your description and recommends the optimal meeting length based on the nature of the topic, best practices, and your past patterns.

3

Review available time slots

PrecisionMeet pulls real-time availability from your calendar and, if you've added guests, theirs too. You see a clean, visual grid of open slots — filtered by your preferences, working hours, and time zone. No more mental calendar math.

4

Confirm and it's done

Select a slot and hit confirm. PrecisionMeet creates the calendar event, sends invitations with a meeting link (Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams), and sets up smart reminders for all attendees. You're done. No follow-up needed.

The AI Engine

How the meeting advisor thinks

Over time, it also learns from your booking history — noticing which meetings tend to run long, which are usually short, and surfacing that context to inform future recommendations.

FAQ

Common questions

Does PrecisionMeet work if my guest uses a different calendar? +
Yes. If your guest also connects their calendar, you'll see combined availability. If they don't, you can still share your availability page and they pick from your open slots — no account required on their end.
Can I customize my working hours? +
Absolutely. Set your working hours, block off focus time, and define buffer periods between meetings. PrecisionMeet will only offer slots within your defined availability window.
Is there a free plan? +
Yes. The free plan includes one calendar connection, the AI meeting advisor, and up to 10 bookings per month. Students with a .edu email get the Pro plan free for up to one year.
What video conferencing tools does PrecisionMeet support? +
PrecisionMeet auto-generates meeting links for Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. Just pick your preferred platform in settings and every booked meeting will include the right link automatically.
How does the AI decide how long a meeting should be? +
The AI analyzes the nature of the meeting from your description — is it a status update, a strategic discussion, a feedback session, an interview? Each type has a recommended range, refined by research on effective meeting lengths. Over time, it also incorporates your personal patterns.
